Accommodation & Meals

We have booked accommodation for everyone on the farm from Thursday night - Sunday morning, February 16-19.

All meals from Friday dinner to Sunday breakfast will be catered, fully Halal and with plenty of vegetarian options. For other meals (from when you arrive on Thursday until lunch on Friday), we recommend coordinating with your group. All rooms have self catering kitchens and a barbecue, so a South African "bring 'n' braai" is a great option. You can also drive to Greyton (30 mins) for meals or groceries.

We will be asking everyone to contribute around R2200 (appx $125) per person for the weekend to help cover accommodation and food costs. We are very grateful for these contributions: please think of this as our wedding gift. Having everyone we love together will be the best gift we could imagine, and we are doing our utmost to make it a magical weekend!

We will contact you with more information once we receive the RSVPs and finalize the rooming.

Theme and fashion

Now the fun stuff: planning outfits! For the wedding day (Saturday) we recommend colorful, summery, formal attire in the cultural style that brings out the best of you.

You can expect the weather at Elandskloof to get to around 30° C (85° F) during the day, and down to about 15° C (60° F) at night. The ceremony, lunch, and reception will all be outdoors, in the shade.

For the wedding ceremony on Saturday, we will be dressed in South Asian style outfits: Anabelle in red and Rafi in white. If you can, please keep these colors reserved for the bride and groom.

On the non-wedding days, feel free to wear what makes you feel most comfortable, keeping in mind that there are many activities at the venue, including swimming and hiking.

Coming from abroad

This gathering will be a truly international affair, with guests arriving from 9 (!!) different countries across 4 continents.

From Rafi's 8 years of working at a travel business, we know that international travel can be both exhilarating and anxiety-provoking. That's why we've put together a handy-dandy logistics guide that will help all international guests feel prepared for their trip.

We also know it's a big holiday for many of you, and you want to experience what South Africa has to offer! We've included some of our favorite tourist activities in case you need some inspiration. We can also help you book accommodation in Cape Town or coordinate activities—just let us know!
